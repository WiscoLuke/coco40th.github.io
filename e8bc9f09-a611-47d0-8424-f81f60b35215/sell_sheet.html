The Verdict

Based on our group criteria (30s-40s, fun but sit-down, classic American fare), Pinewood Social is the ultimate recommendation. It hits all the culinary notes while offering an unbeatable lively atmosphere and the option to bowl afterwards, making it the perfect memorable anchor for a 40th birthday trip!
